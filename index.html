Game Rules

step 1 : click start button

step 2 : when you click start button, the game will start and a color will be flash

step 3 : click that flash color

step 4 : now you enter the level 1

step 5 : now again a color will be flash

step 6 : dont click that flash color

step 7 : click starting flash color than click the new flash color

step 8 : now you enter the level 2

step 9 : now again a color will be flash

step 10 : repeat the same rule

Example

start -> computer flash suppose red

now click red , now computer flash suppose yellow

now click red than yellow, now computer flash suppose green

now click red than yellow than green, now computer flash suppose purple

remember your sequence and reach more level
